What We Do

Learn More What We Do And Get Involved

Maternal Health Care Support Network

Providing life-saving interventions during pregnancy and childbirth. Through community outreach events, we ensure mothers have access to emergency support, blood donations, and timely medical care to prevent deaths.

Learn More

Health Screenings & Mobile Clinics

We conduct on-site screenings for blood pressure, hemoglobin levels, blood grouping, random blood sugar (RBS), and urinalysis. These early checks help identify high-risk pregnancies before complications arise.

Learn More

Maternal Education & Follow Up

Teaching mothers danger signs in pregnancy, birth preparedness, and emergency planning. We empower women with knowledge to protect themselves and their babies, ensuring safer childbirth for all mothers.
