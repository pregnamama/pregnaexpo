Maternal Health Care – Step by Step

1.Registration & Initial Check

The mother’s details are recorded, and basic health information is collected.

2.Health Screening

  • Blood pressure
  • Hemoglobin (Hb)
  • Blood sugar Levels
  • Blood Grouping
  • Urinalysis

3.Obstretic Ultrasund Scan

A basic ultrasound is done to check the baby’s growth and detect any risks.

4.Risk Assessment

The health team identifies any high-risk conditions, like high blood pressure, anemia, or complications in pregnancy.

5.Education & Counseling

Mothers are taught warning signs to watch for. They receive guidance on birth preparedness and emergency planning.

6.Treatment & Referrals

Any problems detected are treated on the spot if possible. High-risk cases are referred to hospitals or specialists for immediate care.

7.Follow-Up & Support

Mothers are advised on follow-up appointments. PregnaMama provides support and guidance until delivery and postpartum care.
