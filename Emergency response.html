How It Works:

1.Identify the emergency

High blood pressure, postpartum bleeding, severe pain, or other urgent maternal complications.

2.Contact PregnaMama support –

    Through our helpline, website, or mobile app.

    3.Get connected immediately

    We link you to the nearest verified hospital, specialist, or ambulance.

    4.Guidance throughout the process

    Our team helps you navigate the emergency, ensures timely treatment, and follows up to support recovery.

    Why This Matters:

    Rapid response is often the difference between life and death. PregnaMama’s emergency network bridges the gap, giving mothers fast access to skilled care, transport, and support, significantly reducing maternal mortality.
