PregnaMama Blood Donor Network

Save Mothers’ Lives, One Donation at a Time

Every day, mothers face life-threatening emergencies during childbirth due to lack of blood. Our Blood Donor Network ensures safe and timely blood is available when it’s needed most.

How It Works

  • ✔ Fill in your details and join the network
  • ✔ Get verified for eligibility and blood type
  • ✔ Receive notifications when a mother needs blood
  • ✔ Donate at a nearby hospital or blood bank
  • ✔ Follow-up to see your donation saved a life

Join the Network

Once you join the PregnaMama Blood Donor Network, you not only help save lives but also gain access to support from other members whenever blood is urgently needed.
